Description

Koi Farm is a game about playing with koi ponds. Explore an infinite number of procedurally generated koi patterns by crossbreeding your fish, and discover new mutations as you go along.

History

Koi Farm was developed by Job Talle over the span of a year. He has a great fascination with procedurally generated content and graphics, which has led to Koi Fam. Together with Samma van Klaarberen (concept art), Harm-Jan Wiechers (audio) and the help of a few translators he was able to self-publish this game to steam on February 26th 2021. The game received an “Overwhelmingly Positive” on Steam. The MacOS port followed on June 21st 2021. A year later, on April 4th 2022 , the game was published on the App Store for iOS. The Android version is TBA, this version is published by Daniël Haitink.

Features

  • Create beautiful koi through selective breeding, and combine fish with interesting patterns and colours to refine them.

  • Store the best koi as collectible trading cards.

  • Let the koi book guide you towards new undiscovered mutations.

  • Generate koi codes: share your koi with other players! Drop koi codes from your friends into your pond.

  • Seasonal in-game events: get into the festivities with your koi!

  • Unwind and enjoy the peaceful gameplay with natural environmental sounds of the interactive scenery.

  • Join the friendly and active community on Discord: participate in challenges and share your koi!
